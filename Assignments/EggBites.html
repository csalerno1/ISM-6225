Bacon and Italian Cheese Egg Bites

This recipe is a great breakfast option that is hearty, filling and mimics the egg bites at Starbucks. Makes 12 egg bites, or 6 servings.


Ingredients

1 & 1/4 Cups 4% Milkfat Cottage Cheese 6 Large Eggs
1 & 1/4 Cups Shredded Italian Cheese 1/4 Teaspoon Salt & Pepper
1 Teaspooon Hot Sauce 1/4 Cup Bacon Bits

Kitchen Needs

Large Bowl Mixer or Blender
Oil Spray (Like Pam) Muffin Pan (Silicone Suggested)
Small Pot or Electric Kettle 9x13 Glass Pan

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit and spray the muffin pan with oil.

2. Boil water using the pot/electric kettle. Fill glass pan halfway with the boiling water.
Place glass pan with water in the oven on the bottom rack to steam the oven.
3. Add cottage cheese, eggs, italian cheese, salt, pepper and hot sauce to the blender/mixing bowl.
Blend until completely smooth, 30 to 45 seconds.

4. Pour the egg mixture evenly into the prepared muffin pan, filling each well to be 3/4 full.
Sprinkle the bacon bits on top of the mixture in the muffin pan well.

5. Bake for 20-23 minutes, or until the egg bites are set.

6. Let the egg bites rest for 5 minutes.
Use a spoon to remove the egg bites from the pan onto a plate.

NOTE: If using a silicon muffin tray, some egg bites may not be fully set.
If this is the case, remove the cooked bites and put the uncooked ones back in the oven for 5-7 minutes.
