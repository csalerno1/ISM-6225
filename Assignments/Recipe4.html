Easy Creamy Chicken Casserole

This recipe is a kid-friendly casserole that is also a great comfort meal. It is easy to prepare and will easily become your family favorite meal. Feeds 4.


Ingredients

1 Pound Chicken Breasts 16 oz Sour Cream
16 oz Cream of Chicken Soup 1 Sleeve Ritz Crackers
Salt & Pepper 1 Can Green Beans (Optional)

Kitchen Needs

Large Bowl Cutting Board
Large Knife Spatula
Large Pan 9X13 Glass Pan
Mixing Spoon Spray Oil (Like Pam)

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

2. Cut chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces. Spray the pan with oil and then place chicken inside.

3. Cook chicken on medium-high heat until cooked through.
Add salt and pepper to taste while cooking.

4. Combine sour cream, cream of chicken, cooked chicken and green beans (optional) in large bowl.
Transfer to 9x13 glass pan.

5. Crush Ritz crackers inside the sleeve wrapper.
Sprinkle the entire sleeve of crushed crackers on the top of the casserole mix.

6. Place glass pan in oven and cook for 30 minutes.

7. Let cool 10 minutes before serving.
