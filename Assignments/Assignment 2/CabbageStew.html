Instant Pot Unstuffed Cabbage Soup

This recipe is a variation of the Polish dish, stuffed cabbage, and is a quick and easy one-pot meal. Goes great with dipping bread.


Ingredients

2 Pounds Lean Ground Beef 1 Whole Medium Sized Cabbage
28oz Whole Crushed Tomatoes 28oz Tomato Sauce
4oz Tomato Paste 4 Cups Low Sodium Beef Broth
1 Medium Onion Olive Oil, drizzle
Oregano Basil
Salt Pepper
16oz Kidney Beans (optional) Sour Cream (for garnish)

Kitchen Needs

Instant Pot Large Stirring Spoon
Cutting Board Large Knife

Instructions

1. Turn the Instant Pot on the saute setting. Add a drizzle of olive oil and let it heat up.

2. Add the ground beef. Season with salt and pepper to your liking. Cook beef, breaking it up and stirring occasionally, until browned.
Turn off the Instant Pot by selecting "Cancel".

3. Slice the onion and cabbage into bite-size pieces.
Add onion to Instant Pot first, then add as much cabbage that will fit in the pot without pushing it down
(Leave a little room at the top)

4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and kidney beans (optional).

5. Add as much beef broth as it takes to fill the pot to the top.

6. Add the oregano, basil, and as much salt and pepper as desired. Stir the mixture.

7. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot. Cook on high pressure for 30 minutes.

8. Once the soup is done cooking, do an instant pressure release.
Add additional seasoning such as salt and pepper as necessary.

9. Dish out the soup and add a small spoonful of sour cream as an optional garnish.
