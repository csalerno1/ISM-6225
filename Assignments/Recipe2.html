Devil's Food Cake Cookies

This recipe is an easy way to make large batches of cookies with only a few ingredients, one of which being a cake mix. The recipe is easy to make your own, as items like peppermint (suggested in the recipe), walnuts or chocolate chips. Makes about 36 cookies.


Ingredients

1 Package Dunkin Hines Devil's Food Cake Mix 2 Large Eggs
1/2 Cup Vegtable Oil 1 Package Candy Canes (Optional)

Kitchen Needs

Large Bowl Mixer or Whisk
2 cookie sheets Spatula
Ziploc Bags (Optional) Something hard and sturdy (ex: metal thermos) (optional)

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and grease the baking sheets.

2. Combine cake mix, eggs and oil in a large bowl. Stir until thoroughly blended.
Shape dough into 36 (1 1/4-inch) balls.
Place dough balls 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheets.

3. (Optional step) While cookies are baking, place candy canes into ziploc bags.
Break the candy canes into small pieces using the hard and sturdy object.

4. Bake cookies in the oven for 10 to 11 minutes.
Cookies will look moist when pulling them out - do not overbake.

5. (Optional Step) Immediately after pulling cookies out of the oven, sprinkle the candy cane pieces on the cookies.

6. Let the cookies cool for 2 minutes.
For chewy cookies, move them off the baking sheets now.
For crispy cookies, wait another 4-5 minutes before moving them off the baking sheet.
